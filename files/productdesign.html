PRODUCT DESIGN

ANAïS ANGELA LEE

Products I've designed and crafted, from start to finish.

PROJECT 1 : JEWELRY

A project I began to continue crafting and gain experience in retail and consumer focused product design.

I use only the best quality materials I can find that suit my designs and meticulously hand-craft each piece. These products are currently on my Etsy shop.

PROJECT 2 : HANDBAGS

I really took a risk with this project. I had no idea where to source the material from, or what to expect when working with leather. Aimed for a unique design with a familiar shape.

I cut and stitched the leather by hand. I have not found a manufacturer to produce the bag but hope to get them made in the near future.

PROJECT 3 : FASHION/RETAIL

This project was inspired by my own love for fashion and the fashion industry. I love to design and sew new clothing inspired by the things I see every day. I haven't pushed this project into a public stage at this time, but hope to soon.
